Summary

"URI: The Surgical Strike" is a 2019 Indian military action film based on true events. It dramatizes the retaliation carried out by the Indian Army in response to the 2016 Uri attack. The film highlights the meticulous planning and execution of the surgical strike, celebrating the bravery and patriotism of Indian soldiers.

Actors

The movie features Vicky Kaushal in the lead role as Major Vihaan Singh Shergill. Supporting roles are played by Paresh Rawal, Yami Gautam, Mohit Raina, and Kirti Kulhari. The cast delivers a compelling and emotional portrayal of real-life heroes.
